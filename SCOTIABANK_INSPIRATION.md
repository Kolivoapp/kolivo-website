# Scotiabank Design Inspiration

## Key Design Elements

### Colors
- **Primary Red:** #EC111A (Scotiabank Red) - Used for CTAs, buttons
- **White Background:** Clean, bright, professional
- **Dark Text:** High contrast for readability
- **Light Gray:** Subtle backgrounds for sections

### Layout
- **Clean white space** - Lots of breathing room
- **Grid-based cards** - Organized content blocks
- **Full-width hero** with image on right, text on left
- **Horizontal tab navigation** for content categories
- **Simple, functional footer**

### Typography
- **Bold headlines** - Clear hierarchy
- **Clean sans-serif** - Professional, readable
- **Moderate sizes** - Not oversized, not small

### Navigation
- **Top bar:** Secondary nav (Personal, Business, Commercial, etc.)
- **Main nav:** Product categories (Bank Accounts, Credit Cards, etc.)
- **Sticky header** on scroll
- **Sign In button** prominent but not overwhelming

### Hero Section
- **Split layout:** Text left, Image right
- **Warm photography** - People, lifestyle
- **Clear value proposition** headline
- **Single CTA button** - "Learn more"
- **Subtle background color** (light green/teal in their case)

### Content Sections
- **Tab-based navigation** for categories
- **Card grid** for features/products
- **Clean borders** and shadows
- **Consistent spacing**

## For KOLIVO™ Adaptation

### Keep from Scotiabank:
- Clean white/light backgrounds (or dark equivalent)
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Split hero layout
- Card-based content organization
- Professional, trustworthy feel
- Simple navigation structure

### Adapt for KOLIVO™:
- Use Navy (#172638) as primary dark color
- Hub Green (#10B981) for accent/CTAs
- KOLIVO Neo Sans typography
- Subtle glass effects (very minimal)
- Dark mode as primary (modern tech feel)
- Product ecosystem showcase

### Avoid:
- Gradient orbs
- Floating animations
- Excessive blur effects
- "AI template" aesthetic
- Overly trendy elements
